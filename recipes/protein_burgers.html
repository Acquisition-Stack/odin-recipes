Healthy Protein Burgers

A High Protein, Delicious Burger With Crispy Fries

Yes, you read that right. You don't have to leave out the fries on this one. You can enjoy this high protein, delicious meal for dinner, or lunch. You consume a high amount of protein, and low fat with this recipe and it should be enough to fill you up until the next day.

Feel free to eat this one several times per week. It goes great with a simple and delicious Salad. If you like your condiments, make sure to pick up a lot fat version of your favourite sauce

Ingredients

  • High protein mince
  • Potatoes
  • Burger buns
  • Cucumber
  • Low fat cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the mince by turning them into 50 gram patties
  2. Fry the mince in a frying pan with olive oil for 3 minutes each side
  3. Add cheese towards the end of cooking. The cheese will begin to melt, yummy.
  4. Put the burgers in the buns and add your favourite sauce
  5. For the fries, go ahead and cut them into thin strips and season with garlic powder, olive oil and some salt/li>
